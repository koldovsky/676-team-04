About Us

Dream Dog is one of the top respected Kennel Clubs in the US focused on defending and promoting the health and well-being of crossbreed and pedigree dogs. We provide dog owners, dog lovers, and trainers with an unmatched source of information and useful advice on dog buying, health, training, feeding, and breeding.

We also offer litter and name registration services, listing your dogs in marketplaces, online record keeping service, and more.
